3. Data Deletion Policy

3.1 Your Right to Erasure

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data held by [Company Name]. This right is recognized under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.

3.2 How to Submit a Deletion Request

You may request deletion of your data through any of the following methods:

  • In-app: Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email: Send a request to privacy@example.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Web form: Submit via our Data Request Form

To verify your identity, we may ask you to confirm ownership of the email address associated with your account.

3.3 Scope of Deletion

Upon a verified deletion request, we will permanently delete or anonymize your account profile and credentials, personal usage history and preferences, payment billing information (excluding legally required transaction records), and communications associated with your identity. Aggregated, anonymized analytics that cannot be attributed to you may be retained.

3.4 Exceptions — Data We May Retain

Certain data may be retained despite a deletion request, only where required or permitted by law:

  • Legal obligations: financial transaction records required by tax or accounting regulations (typically 7–10 years depending on jurisdiction)
  • Fraud prevention: minimal identifiers necessary to prevent re-registration by banned users
  • Ongoing legal proceedings: data subject to a court order or active litigation hold
  • Backup systems: data in encrypted backup snapshots, purged within 90 days as part of our regular backup rotation cycle

3.5 Processing Timeline

We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours of receipt. Deletion will be completed within 30 days for standard requests, 45 days if an extension is necessary (we will notify you in advance), and 90 days for data in backup snapshots. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

3.6 Account vs. Data Deletion

Closing your account does not automatically delete all personal data — it only deactivates access. To ensure full data erasure, you must explicitly submit a deletion request as described in Section 3.2.

3.7 Third-Party Processors

When we process your deletion request, we will also instruct our sub-processors to delete your personal data from their systems. Processors are required to confirm deletion within 30 days.

3.8 Complaints & Escalation

If you are not satisfied with how we handled your deletion request, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., the Bayerisches Landesamt für Datenschutzaufsicht in Bavaria, or the supervisory authority of your EU member state).
